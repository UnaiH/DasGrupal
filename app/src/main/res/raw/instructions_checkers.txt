Checkers consists of only one type of piece, a checker. At the beginning, you can only move one
checker one diagonal space forward during a regular turn. If your checker is in the diagonal space
nearest to one of your opponent's checkers, then you can jump and capture that checker.
To capture a checker, move two diagonal spaces in the direction of the checker
you’re attacking, like you are hopping over your opponent's piece. The space on the
other side of your opponent’s checker has to be empty so that you can move into it. To crown a
checker and make it a crowned checker. This piece can move forward and backward diagonally on the
dark squares, so it's easier for crowned checkers to capture your opponent's checkers
